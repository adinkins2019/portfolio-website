Intro

Welcome to my website, a brief glimpse into the varied eclectic person that I am. Life is about the process of discovery, a constant refinement, being tried in the fire and seeing how things turn out. It's about growth and maturation. It's both the simple and complex, the fragrant flowers that bloom during the spring and the torrential hurricanes that torment the summer. This page will depict some of the moments. While you're perusing this site, please be sure to check out the awesome projects that I've worked on. You won't be disappointed.

About

Nestled in the rural sections of South Carolina, but not far from the metropolitan areas of Columbia, SC and Florence, SC lies a small town name Bishopville, capital seat of Lee County, a place steeped with tradition, history, and folk lore.

I am a father of two sons: Isaac and Ezekiel, a member of Victory Outreach Mission, a nondenominational church, and a devoted husband.
